Definition: Abstract

Creativity

  • The Air - Wind
  • The Abstract Mind
  • The Soft Mind
  • The Inner Child
  • The Playfull
  • The Random / Far Away Connector

All these are different names for the

same aspect: 'Creativity'


In it's purity,

This aspect is a transparent crystal ball

Through which any other color,

color as a metaphor, can shine

>

Definition: Logic

It's the Ability to Combine Any / Every:

Experience:

  • Instincts
  • Emotions
  • Thoughts
  • Perceptions

Stories said Through:

  • Music
  • Images
  • Animations
  • Videos
  • Verbal Communication
  • Books
  • etc.

=> Into an original output


The above not being 'original',

They are experiences of any kind

That already where experimented

Could potentially be

Talent: Skill & Creativity

'Talent' has 2 main aspects

Regarding a ceeative work


Skill & Creativity


For example, you could be a great

Guitarist / Violonist

Unmesuable skill

And play very complicated and complex Works


But that doesn't mean that you can

necessarily create a new song of

that beauty or emotional complexity


Or


Easily seen in children foremost,


Your imagination could be off the charts

The story (for example) created

Might be extremly creative


But that doesn't mean that this story

Will translate it's beuty / complexity

In one draw-ing made by a 5 year old

>

Application: Hobby

In order to generate what you want

One or the other (skill/creativity)

Might necessitate some work


Both, Yes Both,

Can be improved upon


At the level of your own enjoyment

Son't let ANYBODY tell you

That you can not generate something

Beautifull / Meaningfull / Deep

>

Application: Art as a Job / Side-Job

Now for this

An ammount of talent is neccesary


As above, it can be developed

But a certain proclivity is required for

  • A very high potential
  • You actual enjoyment of the activity

For, if you don't enjoy do-ing

that certain work at least 5 hours

or half a day everyday

This would be just another job

Why even bother try-ing

to make it financially viable


An extra activity as a nice hobby

Is a thing to be cherished

And will be with you

Till you're final days

>

Application: Industrial

This implies having firstly a strong

Logic - Intelectual side


For the complexity of the system

To which you are try-ing to improve upon

Must be thoroughly understood


This doesn't truly require an extreme

Raw creative capacity

And it might actually be an annoyance

If you have one


For a very complex established system

Is very rarely changed fundamentally

From the bottom up

Family: What to Consider & Expect

>

Situation: Traumas & Bad Experiences

This is NOT a pet peeve

And is NOT specific to any

"Type of person"


But i do hate that this happens to people

So

Trauma and bad experieces

Will bend, change or plague

Your vision of a familial life


In this scenario traumas & bad experiences,

Regarding familial situations


For the this

The chapter "Past" is

Very helpfull and much

More detailed than this overview

>

Situation: Different Types of People

Conservative types

Will always play the duty card

when it comes to family


If you're a liberal type

this are the aspects where you should focus

To have an accurare representation


Of what starting a family

MEANS TO YOU

>

Expect: A Projection of the Past

At least till your teen years

You couldn't really choose what to do

So the familiai environment

Where you were raised

Would have been 'just there'

Not in your control


It's automatic that we see our new

familial environment

that we build ourselves

as similar

To our past one


But you are not your father/mother

and your spuse is not your mother/father


So that can't be true

Yet still, this plagues the mind of everyone

Especially before starting or deciding to start

A family of their own


But there are practical solutions to this

It starts in the past

Where you will think about & write about

About the semnificative interactions

or the lack these interactions

>

Consider: Envisioning the Future

The best way to fix your vision of the future

is, well, to envision it & write it down

but not only the practical steps/aspects,

especially in regards to your new family


For those don't really build enthuziasm

Those are the chores rather

That can be satisfing, but not exciting


Think about & make a list

with what you'll enjoy doing with

your new son/daugther


Think Hard

Think Deep

Write every one of them down

Then categories (subchapters) will emerge

from these thoughts and pieces of written information


So that you'll see exactly

How and why

Your version of the familial life

Will suit you and will be wounderfull

>

Do: Developing yourself: Before-During

You can prepare what you would:

  • Want to be
  • Want to become
  • Would like to do with your kids
  • and the family as a whole

But don't get frozen up in an endless list

A kid is firstly small


So this advanced building of yoursel

Can very well go

At the same time with the kids growing up


Do your best till you start the family

Continue after, for development is eternal

Maybe not in regards to the body

But rather regarding skills & knowledge


So if everything is not yet in line

You will have time to develop it later


This is not an 'end'

It's a continuation & a new start


You're still you and you're gonna

pursuit what you want, the relevant aspects

And you're gonna have a little thing

That will be joy in your life


Is that so bad?
